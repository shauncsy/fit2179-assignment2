NBL vs NBA Basketball Visualisation

This data visualisation project compares the Australian National Basketball League with the NBA, focusing on teams, locations, player performance, and league development.

Project Introduction

Basketball is becoming increasingly popular in Australia. This project uses interactive visualisations to explore how the NBL compares with the NBA in terms of geography, team structure, and player performance.

Visualisation 1: NBL Team Locations

This map shows NBL team locations across Australia and New Zealand using the prepared NBL team statistics dataset. The number inside each point represents the team's calculated ranking, while the colour represents win percentage. Users can hover over each point to view team performance details such as wins, losses, shooting percentage, rebounds, assists, and turnovers.

Visualisation 2: NBL Offensive and Defensive Performance Map

This map explores NBL team performance from offensive and defensive perspectives. Users can switch between offensive score, defensive score against, and score difference. This helps compare not only where teams are located, but also how they perform on court.

Section 2: NBL Team Statistical Analysis

This section uses the NBL team statistics dataset to compare teams beyond their geographic locations. It focuses on ranking teams by key performance indicators such as win percentage, score, shooting efficiency, rebounds, and assists.

Visualisation 3: NBL Team Metric Ranking

This interactive bar chart ranks NBL teams by different performance metrics. Users can switch between win percentage, average score, field goal percentage, three-point percentage, rebounds, assists, and turnovers to compare team strengths from different perspectives.

Visualisation 4: NBL Offensive vs Defensive Efficiency

This scatter plot compares each NBL team's offensive and defensive performance. The x-axis shows average score, while the y-axis shows average score against. Teams further to the right have stronger scoring ability, while teams lower on the chart allow fewer points. Circle colour represents win percentage, and circle size represents total rebounds.

Visualisation 5: NBL Team Performance Profile Heatmap

This heatmap compares NBL teams across multiple performance indicators, including win percentage, scoring, shooting efficiency, rebounds, assists, turnovers, and defensive score against. Darker colours indicate stronger relative performance. For score against and turnovers, lower values are treated as better performance.
